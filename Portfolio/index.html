About me

Web Developer | Robotics Instructor | Tie-Dye Designer

Hi! I am Idris Bamidele, a web designer/developer, robotics instructor and tie-dye designer focused on crafting great web designs. Designing and Coding have been my passion since the days I started working with computers but I found myself into robotics since 2015. I represented my school -> Lagos State Model College, Meiran at the World Robots Olympiad 2018 Nationals emerging the winner and also went on representing my country -> Nigeria at the finals in Thailand. I enjoy Creating beautifully designed, intuitive and functional websites. When I create websites, I work with technologies such as HTMl, CSS and JavaScript. I can help you develop a fitting design for your new website. I also create beautiful tie-dye designs on fabric which I learnt from my secondary school days. With my knowledge in robotics, I have been able to work with Arc-Lights Foundation in training pupils in the elementary, junior high and senior high schools.
